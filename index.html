Ox tail

Ingredients

  • Ox tail
  • 2 carrots
  • 1-2 sticks celery
  • 2-3 big tomatoes
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 300 ml tomatoe paste
  • 1-2 yellow onion
  • 1-2 shallots
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1 cup aprox chicken stock*

How to

Marinate the ox with salt and pepper, then heat the pan on high and sear the ox tail with a bit of oil all sides, take away!
Dice the vegetable, carrots, celery, tomatoes (skin peeled), slice the onions Sear the opinion and shallots, then add the tomato paste, the last ones go the carrots and the celery, sear the well.
In a large pot put the ox tail and cover with the sauce, chicken stock (hot) and the tomatoes cut in 4. Turn the fire to high until boiling, once boiling add a rock brown sugar and a bit of salt and the wine
Turn the fire to low and cook for 3 hours.After 3 hours add the beans and cook for another 5-10 min.

Serve with rice or favourite side

Brownie II*

* only three ingridients

Ingridients

  • 200g chocolate
  • 250g butter
  • 7-8 eggs

How to

Greasse very well the pan/tray with butter. Melt the choclolate, preferably on the pot with boiling water and the buttter.

Seprate the yolks from the whites. Beat the whites until a consistent foam. Mix the melted, chilled choclolate with the egg yolks and then add the mixture into the foam, gently mixing it. Make sure to keep the foamy texture of the batter. Pour it into the pan/tray and bake at 180 C until clean tooth pick, around 20-30 min

Mung beens curry

Ingridients

  • 4 Tbsp canola oil or other neutral-flavoured oil
  • 1 Tbsp whole cumin seeds
  • 9 cloves garlic crushed (about 3 Tbsp crushed garlic)
  • 4 oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 2 Tbsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 cup mung beans picked over for stones and well rinsed
  • 14 oz can coconut milk
  • 1-2 medium limes juiced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro chopped

How to

1. In a large pot (I like to use my Dutch oven) heat the cooking oil over medium-high heat.

2. Add the cumin seeds and cook for about 1 minute, until they just begin to darken.

3. Add the crushed tomatoes and stir to combine, then add ginger, coriander, turmeric, salt, and cayenne.

4. Saut√© this mixture for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.

5. Add the water and mung beans. Increase the heat to high and bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce to low, cover, and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring the mixture once or twice.

6. Taste a few mung beans to make sure they are cooked. If they are, stir in the coconut milk, and increase the heat to medium-high.

7. Once the curry comes to the boil turn off the heat. Stir in the cilantro and the juice of one lime.

8. Taste and decide whether you'd like to add the second lime as well.

9. Serve hot..
