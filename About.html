Why Cooking

Until that day, until that smile and that smell. It was him, the fucking boy that turned upside down everything I was believing in

So now I love to cook, I love to paint my nails, I like my long hair and I fucking know how to make pickles!

It all started by the desire to do something for this little cute, fucking hot and amazing man I met, it all started with one dinner, I thought, "Just one, then it will let me go", but no, after one year and few months, I am still on it

Now I am even making my website about cooking, cause, apparently I love it!......aaaand I did not want to lose or forget my favourite recipes.... Or maybe I just love to eat and it's nothing to do with this little man
